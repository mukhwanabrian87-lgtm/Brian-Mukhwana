Biography

Civil Engineer & Pedagogue

Fusing real-world construction site experience with standard TVET classroom pedagogy.

As a Civil Engineer, my career is anchored in creating structures that stand the test of time. From comprehensive foundation design to concrete mix audits and structural calculations, I treat every project as a commitment to public safety and engineering excellence.

My engineering work is complemented by my role as a TVET Educator. In the vocational education sector, I focus on bridging the gap between theoretical drawing boards and practical masonry, surveying, and site operations. By implementing Competency-Based Education and Training (CBET), I ensure that my students enter the construction sector as immediate, high-value assets.
